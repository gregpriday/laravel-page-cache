Getting Things Done Summary

By David Allen
1-minute read
business processes self-help personal development
Listen to Audio
business processes self-help personal development

Do you feel anxious and inundated by all of the incomplete tasks hanging over you? Getting Things Done, gives us a system for organizing and managing all the to-do's that threaten to overwhelm us. David Allen’s ideas are timeless and trustworthy, with five steps to master workflow, boost productivity, and reduce our stress. Allen is a productivity consultant and he developed the 'Getting Things Done' system in 2011. He has global acclaim for his work on self-management and productivity, and has coached some of the brightest and busiest people around. In this book he shares some of his tips on what helps his clients, knowing that most of us are overwhelmed by heavy workloads.
